Finished! To start your hike, you just need to check your inputs here and then press "Forward". A confirmation dialog will ensure that you actually want to start the time observation.

On top are the hike times, just the endtime is required.

Your data are on the top left, beside the scheduled alert times to the right.

Below you find the mail subject and the full mail text, including your route, including attached files. Nevertheless, if you want to change something, you need to navigate back.

On bottom you see, if you chose to use it, the Continue-to-next mail.

When you navigate forward from here, you will be asked whether you want to activate the hike later via a start e-mail or right now.

  • If you click "Later", you can activate the observation remotely when you actually arrived at your hiking trail safely. You would receive a reminder-mail and can activate the hike via a reply to it. That reply must contain the MAIL-ID either in text or as attachment.
  • If you click "Now", the observation would start immediately, and alerts would be sent in vain if you had an accident on the way to your hiking trail.
