Create the text of your alert e-mail carefully. The "E-Mail Subject" should indicate the urgency of the situation.

The e-mail text will consist of three parts. The "Alert E-Mail Text" gives the introduction below the automatically created salutation on top.

To avoid excited confusion, you then should tell the contact person what to do, step by step. Use the "+" and "-" buttons to add and remove steps, edit their texts in the field below. The steps will be numbered automatically by the apliction, you can see that on the final "Activation" page.

Optionally there will be the "Continue to next" e-mail. This is sent when to any contact that did not respond in the expected wait-time. It is useful to avoid a whole chain of people trying to organize help.
