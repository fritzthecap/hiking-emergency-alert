The time observation of your hike is now running. The application will output control messages in the green (progress) and red (errors) text areas.

It is possible that you immediately get an error, for example when the mail/internet connection got lost meanwhile, so better have a look before you set off.

Do not shut down your computer, do not set it to sleep, do not turn off the modem!

The window can not be closed now, you need to press the "Home Again" button. A dialog will ask if you really want to terminate.

Afterwards you can either start a new hike, when you were back in time, or have a look at the passed one. The window can be closed then again.
