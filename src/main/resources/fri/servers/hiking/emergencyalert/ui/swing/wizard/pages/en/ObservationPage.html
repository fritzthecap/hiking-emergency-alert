The time observation of your hike is now running. The application will write messages in the green (progress) and red (errors) text areas.

It is possible that you immediately get an error, for example when the mail/internet connection broke down, so better have a look at these consoles before you set off.

Do not shut down your computer, do not set it to sleep- or hibernation-mode, and do not turn off the modem!

The application can not be closed now, you need to press the "Home Again" button. A dialog will then ask if you really want to terminate.

Afterwards you can either start a new hike when you were back in time, or have a look at the passed one, or close the window.
